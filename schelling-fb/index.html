Schelling coordination game with false belief

Bob believes that Alice wants to meet him, and Alice knows this, but in fact Alice wants to avoid Bob (Schelling 1960).

(define (sample-location)
  (if (flip .55)
      'popular-bar
      'unpopular-bar))

(define (alice* depth)
  (query
   (define alice*-location (sample-location))
   alice*-location
   (not (equal? alice*-location (bob (- depth 1))))))

(define (alice depth)
  (query
   (define alice-location (sample-location))
   alice-location
   (equal? alice-location (bob (- depth 1)))))

(define (bob depth)
  (query
   (define bob-location (sample-location))
   bob-location
   (or (= depth 0)
       (equal? bob-location (alice depth)))))

(alice* 5)

Schelling, Thomas C. 1960. The Strategy of Conflict. Harvard University Press.
