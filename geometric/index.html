Geometric Distribution

A simple recursively defined distribution on the integers.

(define (geometric p)
  (if (flip p)
      0
      (+ 1 (geometric p))))

(geometric .5)

;; Alternatively, we can write the geometric distribution in
;; tail-recursive form:

(define (geometric-alt p n)
  (if (flip p)
      n
      (geometric-alt p (+ n 1))))

(geometric-alt .5 0)
